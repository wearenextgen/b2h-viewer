# B2H Icon Requirements

The generated files already contain working inline SVG icons. No additional icon files are technically required.

If custom branded SVGs are created, use this complete icon list.

## Shared Interface Icons

- Product rotation / 3D interaction
- Formula or ingredients
- Quality / shield
- Purity / sparkle or droplet
- Daily routine / calendar
- Timing / clock
- Meal / plate
- Tablet or capsule

## Omega-3

- Fish or fish oil
- Heart
- Brain
- Eye / vision
- Triglycerides / blood-lipid chart
- Purity / quality shield
- EPA and DHA molecule or paired droplets
- Capsule

## Multivitamin Complex

- Energy / lightning bolt
- Fatigue reduction / battery
- Immune support / shield
- Cell protection / antioxidant cell
- Bones and muscles
- Multivitamin tablet
- Vitamins and minerals
- Daily routine

## Magnesium Ultra Complex

- Fatigue reduction / energy
- Nervous system / neuron or brain
- Muscle function
- Energy metabolism
- Magnesium Bisglycinate / mineral
- Bioavailability / absorption
- Zinc and Vitamin B6
- Tablet after a meal

## Recommended SVG Treatment

- Single-color line icons
- Rounded line caps and corners
- Transparent background
- Consistent `64 × 64` viewBox
- Brown and white checkerboard variants
- Avoid detailed illustrations that become unreadable on mobile
